Our Story

Welcome to Santa Monica, where passion meets tradition in every slice. Since 2020, we've been crafting authentic Italian pizzas with love and dedication, bringing the true taste of Italy to Manchester.

Our journey began with a simple dream: to create the perfect pizza experience. Today, we're proud to serve our community with the finest ingredients, traditional recipes, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Every pizza we make is a reflection of our commitment to quality and authenticity. From our hand-tossed dough to our homemade sauces, we ensure that every bite brings you closer to the heart of Italy.
